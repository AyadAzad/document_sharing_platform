Privacy Policy

At Komar Document Sharing, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

1. Data Collection

We collect only the necessary information to provide our services, such as your name, email address, and uploaded documents. We do not collect sensitive information without your explicit consent.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to provide, maintain, and improve our services. We may also use your email address to send important updates about your account or the platform.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard encryption methods, including Advanced Encryption Standard (AES) and Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC), to protect your documents and personal data from unauthorized access.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties without your consent, except where required by law. Any third-party services we integrate with are carefully vetted for privacy compliance.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data at any time. For more information, or to make a request, contact our support team at komar@komar.edu.iq .
