About Our Project

Welcome to our Document Sharing Platform, a secure and innovative solution designed for seamless file sharing among university staff and other professionals. Our mission is to provide a user-friendly platform that ensures the highest level of security and confidentiality in document exchange.


Who We Are

We are a team of passionate developers and researchers committed to revolutionizing secure document sharing by integrating Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC) and Advanced Encryption Standards (AES). Our platform leverages cutting-edge cryptographic techniques to safeguard your files against unauthorized access, even in the era of quantum computing. Our Vision To create a reliable, efficient, and future-ready document-sharing system that serves as a benchmark for secure digital collaboration in academic and professional settings.
